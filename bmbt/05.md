### Set Brightness Example

    3B 06 F0 05 41 01 6C E4
    3B 06 F0 05 42 01 6C E7

### Request Brightness Example

    3B 05 F0 05 40 01 8A


## Parameters

Fixed length. One byte field.

The MK4 uses following brightness steps:

    0  = FF
    1  = EC
    2  = E0
    3  = D4
    4  = C8
    5  = BC
    6  = B0
    7  = A4
    8  = 98
    9  = 8C
    10 = 80
    11 = 0C
    12 = 18
    13 = 24
    14 = 30
    15 = 3C
    16 = 48
    17 = 54
    18 = 60
    19 = 6C
    20 = 7F
