# `0x4a` BMBT Tape/LED Control

Radio `0x68` â†’ BMBT `0xf0`

This command is used to control the radio indicator and tape drive.

### Related Commands

- `0x4b` BMBT Tape Status

### Example Frames
    68 04 F0 4A 00 D6   # LED Off
    68 04 F0 4A FF 29   # LED On

    68 04 F0 4A 40 96   # Eject Tape

    68 04 F0 4A 4A 9C   # Tape Stop
    68 04 F0 4A 4B 9D   # Tape Play

For detailed examples, see below!

## Parameters

The one and only argument is a control byte.\
No bitfields this time!\
The command bytes are hardcoded in the radio's firmware.

### LED Off (0x00)
Turns the radio indicator off. 

    68 04 F0 4A 00 D6

### LED On (0xFF)
Turns the radio indicator on, duh. 

    68 04 F0 4A FF 29

### Eject Tape (0x40)
Flips down the screen and ejects the tape.

    68 04 F0 4A 40 96

### Dolby Mode B (0x5D)
Turns on Dolby B noise reduction.

    68 04 F0 4A 5D 8B

### Dolby Mode C (0x5E)
Turns on Dolby C noise reduction.

    68 04 F0 4A 5E 88

### Dolby Mode Off (0x5F)
Turns off Dolby noise reduction.

    68 04 F0 4A 5F 89

### Tape Side 1 (0x5A)
Switches to tape side 1.\
You have to send a second command (0x41) to actually execute the action.

    68 04 F0 4A 5A 8C
    68 04 F0 4A 41 97  # Execute

### Tape Side 2 (0x5B)
Switches to tape side 2.\
You have to send a second command (0x41) to actually execute the action.

    68 04 F0 4A 5B 8D
    68 04 F0 4A 41 97  # Execute

### Forward (0x44)
Forwards the tape. Can be canceled with the 0x41 command.
    
    68 04 F0 4A 44 92
    68 04 F0 4A 41 97  # Cancel

### Rewind (0x45)
Rewinds the tape. Can be canceled with the 0x41 command.
    
    68 04 F0 4A 45 94
    68 04 F0 4A 41 97  # Cancel

### Forward (0x44)
Forwards the tape. Can be canceled with the 0x41 command.
    
    68 04 F0 4A 44 92
    68 04 F0 4A 41 97  # Cancel

### Fast Forward (0x42)
Fast forwards the tape. Can be canceled with the 0x41 command.
    
    68 04 F0 4A 42 94
    68 04 F0 4A 41 97  # Cancel

### Fast Rewind (0x43)
Fast rewinds the tape. Can be canceled with the 0x41 command.
    
    68 04 F0 4A 43 95
    68 04 F0 4A 41 97  # Cancel

### Play (0x4B)
Starts to play the tape.

    68 04 F0 4A 4B 9D

### Stop (0x4A)
Stops the tape.
    
    68 04 F0 4A 4A 9C

### Stop/Mode Change (0x48)
Also stops the tape.\
The radio sends this and 0x4A when switching to another mode.\
If you just turn off the radio, only 0x4A will be sent.
    
    68 04 F0 4A 48 9E

### Commit/Cancel (0x41)
I'm not 100% sure what you could call this command.\
It cancels rewind and forward, commits the tape side an even starts to play the tape if it is currently stopped.

    68 04 F0 4A 41 97

### Unknown (0x80)
    
    68 04 F0 4A 80 56

### Unknown (0x90)
The radio sends this when you turn on the radio or switch to tape mode.

    
    68 04 F0 4A 90 56
